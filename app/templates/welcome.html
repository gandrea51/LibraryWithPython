How to get there

We are easily accessible by various means of transport. Here’s how:

By Car

Once you have taken the A13 motorway exit Ferrara Sud, continue towards Ferrara Centro. When you reach Via Darsena, at the roundabout, take Corso Isonzo. We are 300 meters on the left.

By Train

From the central station, take line 1, 6 or 9. Get off at Cavour Isonzo and continue for 500 meters on Corso Isonzo. You will find us on the right. Or go for Corso Piave and at the roundabout with Via Piagipane for Corso Isonzo and proceed for 100 meters. You’ll find us on the right.

By Bus

From the central station, take line 1, 6 or 9. Get off at Cavour Isonzo and continue for 500 meters on Corso Isonzo. We are on the right.
